GapShap Child Safety Standards

Our Commitment to Child Safety

GapShap is committed to protecting children and preventing child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) on our platform. We take this responsibility seriously and have implemented comprehensive safety measures to ensure a secure environment for all users.

Safety Measures & Prevention

  • Age Verification: We require users to provide accurate age information and verify their identity where possible.
  • Content Moderation: Our platform employs automated systems and human moderators to detect and remove inappropriate content.
  • User Reporting: Easy-to-use reporting mechanisms allow users to flag concerning content or behavior immediately.
  • Automated Detection: Advanced algorithms help identify and prevent the sharing of inappropriate content.
  • Regular Audits: We conduct regular safety audits and reviews of our systems and policies.
  • Community Guidelines: Clear community standards that prohibit harmful behavior and content.

Reporting Child Safety Concerns

GapShap provides multiple ways for users to report child safety concerns:

  • In-App Reporting: Users can report concerns directly through our app's reporting system
  • 24/7 Response: All reports are reviewed promptly, typically within 24 hours
  • Appropriate Action: We take immediate action on verified reports, including content removal and account suspension
  • Law Enforcement: We cooperate fully with law enforcement when required

Legal Compliance

GapShap complies with all relevant child safety laws and regulations, including:

  • Reporting to regional and national authorities as required by law
  • Cooperation with law enforcement investigations
  • Maintenance of records as required by applicable regulations
  • Regular review and updates of our safety policies
